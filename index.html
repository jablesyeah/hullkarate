Welcome to Karate Hull

At Karate Hull we practice a style of karate called Shukokai. As a club we are well established in East Yorkshire and Humberside. Training at Karate Hull is a disciplined & fun environment, where as a student you can develop confidence, improved co-ordination and a greater level of personal fitness as well as learning this fascinating martial art.

For ages 6 and up

We normally take children from the age of six. We have had many students who do not take karate up until they are 40's and 50's who have still gone on to achieve black belt level. From as young as 5 and our eldest student at 59 theres no excuse not to give us a go!
